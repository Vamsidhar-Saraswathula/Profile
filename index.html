About

I'm a Computer Vision Researcher at Auric AI Labs, Bangalore, specializing in data‑constrained learning: few‑shot, zero‑shot, self‑supervised, and unsupervised techniques for building scalable, intelligent visual systems. At Auric AI, I lead fine‑grained object detection for defense applications, from concept through to MVP.

My research interests span image processing, computer vision, and deep learning, with particular depth in image deblurring and super‑resolution under limited or absent training data.
